Meet The Team

Formed in 2003, Dent Control was built by car people who simply believe in doing it right. Cars are our passion, and our commitment to providing unmatched quality and stellar customer service at a fair price is unmatched.

John Doe

John Doe has been working in dent repair for the last 20 years. He founded his career from a love of cars, as well an awareness of what really goes on beneath the hood of a car. Born in Topeka Kansas, John grew up in the auto boom of the 60's. Seeing the growth of new, exciting and innovative car designs first-hand, John learned early-on how to tinker, take apart and repair all kinds of cars. Honing and bulding on these skills over the years, John is still just as passionate about cars now as ever.
